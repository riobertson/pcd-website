Split Tips

💡 Equal — Same amount each payment. Great for predictability.


💡 Front-Loaded — Pay more in the first payments. Good if you expect less income later.


💡 Back-Loaded — Pay less now, more later. Useful when money is tight right now but you expect more income soon.


⚠️ Only bills marked as "Can Split" will appear in the selector. Edit a bill to enable splitting.
