Anointed Leadership

Meet Our Leaders

God has blessed Purpose Church with Spirit-filled leaders whose hearts beat for His people — from brokenness into fullness of purpose.

Founding Pastors

Head Pastor & First Lady

Pastor Michael Herron
Senior Pastor & Founder
Senior Pastor & Founder
Pastor Michael Herron

Pastor Michael Herron received the call of God after seven years of faithful service in youth ministry and worship at Believers International Church. Obedient to the voice of God, he and First Lady Mal planted Purpose Church of Denton beginning with a single home Bible study.

On their very first night, Pastor Mike was led to deliver a word about Purpose — and Purpose Church was born. His anointed leadership, heart for people, and uncompromising commitment to the Word of God have guided Purpose Church through over a decade of Spirit-filled ministry and growth.

"
We will love God's people from brokenness to restoration and victory. That is our mission, our calling, and our commitment.
— Pastor Michael Herron
First Lady Mal Herron
Co-Pastor & First Lady
Co-Pastor & First Lady
First Lady Mal Herron

First Lady Mal Herron has been a cornerstone of Purpose Church since its very first night. Serving alongside Pastor Mike as a gifted Praise and Worship Leader before the founding of Purpose Church, her heart for God's people and her deep commitment to worship helped shape the spiritual culture of Purpose Church from day one.

First Lady Mal leads with grace, love, and a genuine care for every member of the Purpose Church family. Her presence is a constant reminder that the Church is built on love, and that every person who walks through the doors matters deeply to God.

"
Every soul that walks through these doors deserves to be met with love, with purpose, and with the transforming power of the Word of God.
— First Lady Mal Herron

Our Doctrine

What We Believe

We believe that if we show love to all people, they will come to know Jesus and begin to walk out and fulfill their God-given purpose in life.

✝
Love must be the foundation of your gift for God's anointing to be entrusted in you for the benefit of His people.
📖
We believe in the unadulterated word of God as the final authority in all matters of faith and life.
☁️
We believe in the Trinity: The Father, The Son, and The Holy Ghost — three persons, one God.
🕊️
We believe that Jesus is God's only begotten Son, sent to redeem all of humanity.
🙏
We believe in the death, burial, and resurrection of Jesus Christ as the cornerstone of our faith.
🌿
We believe in the five-fold ministry: Apostles, Prophets, Evangelists, Pastors, and Teachers. (Ephesians 4:11)
⚓
We believe in the restoration and building up of God's people through community, discipleship, and love.
🔑
We believe that faith is the key to unlocking your destiny and fulfilling everything God has placed inside of you.
☀️
We believe that the joy of the Lord is our strength and it should be exhibited in every area of our lives. (Nehemiah 8:10)

"Empowering People for Purpose"

At Purpose Church of Denton, everything we do flows from these core beliefs. Our purpose, mission, and vision are all grounded in the Word of God and expressed through the love we show to every person who walks through our doors.
