Last updated: June 17, 2026

These Terms & Conditions govern the text messaging (SMS) program operated by Purpose Church of Denton ("we," "us," or "our"). By opting in, you agree to these terms.

Program Description

Purpose Church of Denton sends text message updates to members and friends of the church who have opted in. Messages may include service reminders, event announcements, ministry updates, prayer-related communications, and general church news.

How to Opt In

You may opt in to receive text messages by providing your mobile phone number on a connect card or sign-up form at the church and indicating your agreement to receive text updates. By providing your number and consent, you authorize Purpose Church of Denton to send recurring text messages to that number.

Message Frequency & Cost

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Your mobile carrier's standard messaging and data charges will apply to any messages you send or receive. Purpose Church of Denton does not charge for the text messages themselves.

Help & Opt-Out

For help or more information, reply HELP to any of our text messages, or contact us at purposechurch77@gmail.com.

To stop receiving text messages at any time, reply STOP to any message. After you reply STOP, you will receive one confirmation message and then no further texts unless you opt in again.

Supported Carriers

Our messaging program is supported by major U.S. mobile carriers. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Privacy

Your privacy matters to us. We do not sell your information, and no mobile information is shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. For full details on how we handle your information, please review our Privacy Policy.
